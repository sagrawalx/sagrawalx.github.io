*A translation of a poem by Harivansh Rai Bachchan from Hindustani*

<pre class="poem">
Here comes the procession of the Sun!

The gilded chariot of newborn sunbeams,
Its floral path blooming before it,
Its cloud-like attendants clad in gold,
Here comes the procession of the Sun!

The birds are a convoy of minstrels,
Singing paeans into the skies,
As the Army of Stars flees the battlefield,
Here comes the procession of the Sun!

I want to leap up, proclaiming "Victory!"
But I falter when I see—
The illustrious King of the Night, now in tatters…
Here comes the procession of the Sun!
</pre>
