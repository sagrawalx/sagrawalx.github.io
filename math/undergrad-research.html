Undergraduate Research

Here is some information for undergraduates who might be interested in doing research with me!

My main mathematical interests are in algebraic geometry and neighboring fields (category theory, commutative algebra, number theory, representation theory, differential geometry, algebraic topology, …). This means that some understanding of algebra is important for a lot of what I like to think about (more specifically, you’ll probably want to know something about vector spaces, groups, rings, and fields).
