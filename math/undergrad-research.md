# Undergraduate Research

Here is some information for undergraduates who might be interested in doing research with me.

My interests are in algebraic geometry and neighboring fields (category theory, commutative algebra, number theory, representation theory, differential geometry, algebraic topology, ...). This means that some understanding of algebra is essential for almost everything I like thinking about. More specifically, if you're interested in working with me on a math research project, you will want to know something about vector spaces (at CC, that's MA 220) and about groups, rings, and fields (at CC, that's MA 321 and possibly MA 421) for most of what's discussed below.
