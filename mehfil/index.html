Focus: Faiz Ahmad Faiz (फ़ैज़ अहमद फ़ैज़ · فیض احمد فیض, 1911–1984)

Faiz was a leading voice of the Progressive Writers’s Movement, and his powerful poetry continues to inspire progressives and revolutionaries in South Asia and around the world today.
