Overview

The Hindi-Urdu Mehfil is a gathering for anyone in the Colorado Springs area* with an interest in Hindi-Urdu linguistics, literature, music, film, etc. In particular, Hindi-Urdu learners are very welcome to participate.

*: In observance of covid precautionary measures, we’ll meet via Zoom until further notice.

  • If you’d like to be on the mailing list, send me an email at to let me know!
  • If you have a topic in mind that you’d like to discuss during one of our meetings, please let me know and I’ll add it to our calendar! Also, here’s an ongoing list of possible future topics, just in case we need it.
