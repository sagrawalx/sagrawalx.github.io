# Talks

Here are slides/notes for some of my past talks. If you find any errors in these, please let me know!

* From category $\mathcal{O}^\infty$ to locally analytic representations. Notes for a talk. Two versions: 
    - [Rennes](/assets/misc/o-infty-rennes.pdf). May 2021 at the *Séminare géométrie arithmétique* at the Institut de recherches mathématique de Rennes. This talk includes more background material compared to the Padova version below. 
    - [Padova](/assets/misc/o-infty-padova.pdf). April 2021 at the *p-adic Representations and Arithmetic D-modules Seminar*, organized by Andrés Sarrazola Alzate and Francesco Baldassari, at the Università degli Studi di Padova. This talk assumes some background material that had been discussed in preceding seminar talks. 
* [Perspective: The Mathematics of Depicting Depth](/assets/misc/perspective.pdf). Slides for a talk I gave at Colorado College's *Fearless Fridays* seminar in February 2020. 
* [A Smörgåsbord of Arithmetic Geometry](/assets/misc/smorgasbord.pdf). Slides for a talk I gave at Colorado College's *Fearless Fridays* seminar in February 2018.
* [Contangent Complex](/assets/misc/cotangent.pdf). Some (incomplete!) notes I compiled when I was preparing for a talk I gave in David Nadler's fall 2013 *Geometric Representation Theory* seminar.
