Other Writing

  • Introduction to Cryptography (2023). Interactive open source introductory text on cryptography.
  • Off on a Tangent (2019). Open source text about several incarnations of the derivative.
  • Deformations of Overconvergent Isocrystals on the Projective Line (2018). PhD Thesis. Contains more expository content than my corresponding publication with the same title.
  • Exponential at Infinity (2017). A fun short and explicit calculation I did during summer 2017 involving an irregular rank 1 overconvergent differential module over the Robba ring.
  • Fundamental Group of a Curve (2012). Expository term paper written for an algebraic geometry course.
  • The Euler Characteristic of the Moduli Space of Stable Maps into a Grassmannian (2011). Undergraduate thesis. Mostly expository.
